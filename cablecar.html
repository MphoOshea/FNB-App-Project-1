Welcome to the cablecar page!

Here you can find information about cablecars.

This is a cablecar.

Cablecars are a type of aerial tramway that uses a cable to pull cars along a suspended track.

They are often used in mountainous areas to transport people and goods over difficult terrain.

Cablecars can be found in ski resorts, tourist attractions, and urban areas.
