This is a truck.

Trucks are motor vehicles designed primarily for transporting cargo.

They come in various sizes and configurations, from small pickup trucks to large heavy-duty trucks.

Trucks are commonly used in industries such as construction, logistics, and transportation.

They can be powered by gasoline or diesel engines and are often equipped with features such as trailers, flatbeds, and cargo compartments.
