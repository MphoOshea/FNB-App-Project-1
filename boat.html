This is a boat.

Boats are watercraft that are designed to float on water and are used for various purposes, including transportation, recreation, and fishing.

Boats come in various shapes and sizes, from small canoes and kayaks to large yachts and cargo ships.

They can be powered by oars, sails, or engines, and are often made from materials such as wood, fiberglass, or metal.

Boating is a popular recreational activity enjoyed by many people around the world.
