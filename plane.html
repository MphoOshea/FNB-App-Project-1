This is a plane.

Planes are powered flying vehicles that are designed for air travel.

They are equipped with wings and engines that allow them to lift off the ground and fly through the air.

Planes come in various sizes and types, including commercial airliners, private jets, and military aircraft.

They are used for transportation of passengers and cargo, as well as for military and recreational purposes.

Flying is a popular mode of travel that allows people to reach distant destinations quickly and efficiently.
